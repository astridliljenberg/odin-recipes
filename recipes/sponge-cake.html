Sponge Cake

Description

This is a recipe for delicious sponge cake.

Ingredients

  • Cake flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Beat the egg whites.
  2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar.
  3. Fold the egg whites into the egg yolk mixture.
  4. Sift in the cake flour and baking poweder and add the oil.
  5. Bake in a pan.
